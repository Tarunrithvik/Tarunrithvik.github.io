### What is a Demat Account?
A demat account is a dematerialized account that allows you to hold your securities in an electronic format. It's a mandatory requirement for trading in the Indian stock market. With a demat account, you can buy and sell securities, and the transactions are settled electronically. This makes the process faster, safer, and more efficient. In India, demat accounts are maintained by depositories such as NSDL and CDSL, which ensure the safekeeping and transfer of securities.

## Requirements for Opening a Demat Account with Zerodha
To open a demat account with Zerodha, you need to meet certain eligibility criteria and provide the required documents.

### Eligibility Criteria
To be eligible for a demat account with Zerodha, you must:
* Be an Indian citizen
* Be at least 18 years old
* Have a valid PAN card
* Have a valid Aadhaar card
* Have a bank account in your name

### Documents Required
The documents required to open a demat account with Zerodha include:
* PAN card
* Aadhaar card
* Proof of address (such as a passport, driving license, or utility bill)
* Proof of income (such as a salary slip or income tax return)
* Bank account statement or cancelled cheque

## Step-by-Step Process to Open a Demat Account Online with Zerodha
Opening a demat account online with Zerodha is a simple and straightforward process. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

### Registration
To register for a demat account with Zerodha, follow these steps:
1. Visit Zerodha's website and click on the "Open an Account" button.
2. Fill in the registration form with your personal details, including your name, email address, and phone number.
3. Verify your email address and phone number by clicking on the links sent to you by Zerodha.

### Verification
To verify your account, you need to provide the required documents and complete the e-KYC process. Here's how:
1. Log in to your Zerodha account and click on the "Verify" button.
2. Upload the required documents, including your PAN card, Aadhaar card, and proof of address.
3. Complete the e-KYC process by entering your Aadhaar number and OTP.

### Account Activation
Once your account is verified, you can activate it by funding it with a minimum amount of ₹1,000. Here's how:
1. Log in to your Zerodha account and click on the "Funds" button.
2. Select the payment method, such as net banking or UPI.
3. Enter the amount you want to deposit and confirm the transaction.

### What is Notion?
Notion is a note-taking and project management tool that allows users to create a personalized workspace with various templates and blocks. It offers a range of features, including databases, calendars, and Kanban boards, making it a versatile tool for teams and individuals alike. However, its versatility can also be a drawback, as it can be overwhelming for new users.

### Limitations of Notion for Project Management
While Notion is an excellent tool for note-taking and personal productivity, it has some limitations when it comes to project management. One of the main limitations is scalability. As teams grow, Notion can become cluttered and difficult to navigate, making it challenging to manage large-scale projects. Additionally, Notion's customization options can be overwhelming, and its lack of built-in project management features, such as Gantt charts and resource allocation, can make it less suitable for complex projects. Indian teams, in particular, may find that Notion's pricing, which starts at ₹499 per user per month, can be steep for large teams. Therefore, many teams are looking for a Notion alternative for project management that can better meet their needs.

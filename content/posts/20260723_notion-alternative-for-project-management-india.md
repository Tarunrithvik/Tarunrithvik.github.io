## Introduction to Notion and Its Limitations
Notion is a popular all-in-one workspace that offers a wide range of features, including notes, tasks, databases, and pages. It's a versatile tool that can be used for personal and professional purposes. As of 2026, Notion has gained immense popularity in India, with many teams and individuals using it for project management. The pricing of Notion in India starts at ₹499 per month for the personal plan, while the team plan costs ₹999 per month.

### What is Notion?
Notion is a cloud-based platform that allows users to create custom workflows, manage tasks, and collaborate with team members. It offers a wide range of templates and blocks that can be used to create custom pages, databases, and workflows. Notion is available on desktop, mobile, and web, making it accessible from anywhere.

### Limitations of Notion for Project Management
While Notion is a powerful tool, it may not be the best fit for every team. Some of the limitations of Notion for project management include:
* Steep learning curve: Notion can be overwhelming for new users, especially those who are not familiar with its unique interface and features.
* Limited reporting and analytics: Notion lacks advanced reporting and analytics features, making it difficult to track project progress and performance.
* Limited integration with other tools: Notion has limited integration with other tools and platforms, which can make it difficult to use it with existing workflows.

## Canva Pricing Plans in India
Canva offers four main pricing plans in India: Free, Pro, Team, and Enterprise. Each plan is designed to cater to different needs and budgets, so let's take a closer look at what each plan has to offer.

### Free Plan
The Free plan is perfect for individuals who want to try out Canva's features without committing to a paid plan. With the Free plan, you get access to:
* Limited templates and design tools
* 1GB of storage
* Limited collaboration features

The Free plan is free, making it an excellent option for those who want to test the waters before upgrading to a paid plan.

### Pro Plan
The Pro plan is designed for individuals and small businesses who need more advanced features and storage. With the Pro plan, you get:
* Unlimited templates and design tools
* 100GB of storage
* Advanced collaboration features
* Custom fonts and logos
* Priority support

The Pro plan costs ₹749 per month (or ₹6,499 per year), making it an affordable option for those who need more features and storage.

### Team Plan
The Team plan is perfect for small teams and businesses who need to collaborate on design projects. With the Team plan, you get:
* All Pro plan features
* 1TB of storage
* Advanced collaboration features
* Custom fonts and logos
* Priority support
* Single sign-on (SSO) integration

The Team plan costs ₹1,799 per month (or ₹19,999 per year) for up to 5 users, making it an excellent option for small teams who need to collaborate on design projects.

### Enterprise Plan
The Enterprise plan is designed for large teams and businesses who need advanced features, custom solutions, and priority support. With the Enterprise plan, you get:
* All Team plan features
* Custom solutions and integrations
* Advanced security and compliance features
* Priority support
* Dedicated account management

The Enterprise plan is custom-priced, so you'll need to contact Canva's sales team for a quote.

When considering **Canva pricing in India**, it's essential to think about your specific needs and budget. By choosing the right plan, you can unlock the full potential of Canva and create stunning designs and graphics that help you stand out.

### Collaboration Tools

### Brand Kit and Customization

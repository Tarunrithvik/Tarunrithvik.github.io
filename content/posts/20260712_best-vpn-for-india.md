## Introduction to VPNs in India
India has become a significant player in the digital world, with over 700 million internet users as of 2026. However, this growth has also led to an increase in online threats, making it essential for Indians to use a Virtual Private Network (VPN) to stay secure online. A VPN is a tool that encrypts your internet traffic, protecting your data from hackers and snoopers. When choosing the best VPN for India, consider the key features and pricing to make an informed decision.

### Why Indians Need a VPN
Indians need a VPN for several reasons:
* **Secure public Wi-Fi**: Public Wi-Fi networks in India are often unsecured, making it easy for hackers to intercept your data. A VPN encrypts your traffic, ensuring your data remains safe.
* **Bypass geo-restrictions**: Many online services, such as Netflix and Amazon Prime, have content that is only available in certain regions. A VPN can help you bypass these restrictions and access your favorite shows and movies.
* **Protect against cyber threats**: India has seen a significant increase in cyber threats, including phishing and ransomware attacks. A VPN can help protect you against these threats by encrypting your traffic and hiding your IP address.

### Key Features to Look for in a VPN

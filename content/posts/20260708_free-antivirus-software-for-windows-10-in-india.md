### Why You Need Antivirus Software
Antivirus software is designed to detect, prevent, and remove malware from your computer. Without it, your PC is exposed to various threats, including viruses, Trojans, spyware, and ransomware. These threats can compromise your personal data, slow down your computer, and even lead to financial losses. In India, where cybersecurity awareness is still growing, it's essential to take proactive measures to protect your digital assets. By installing **free antivirus software for Windows 10**, you can significantly reduce the risk of malware infections and keep your PC running smoothly.

### Key Features to Look for in Free Antivirus Software
When choosing free antivirus software, there are several key features to look for:
* Real-time protection: This feature scans your computer continuously, detecting and blocking threats in real-time.
* Virus scans: Regular virus scans help identify and remove existing malware from your computer.
* Firewall integration: A built-in firewall helps block unauthorized access to your computer and network.
* Automatic updates: Regular updates ensure your antivirus software stays up-to-date with the latest threat definitions and security patches.
* Additional features: Some antivirus software options offer additional features, such as VPN integration, password management, and online backup.
